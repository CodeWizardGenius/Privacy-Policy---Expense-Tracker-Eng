Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 22, 2026

The Expense & Subscription Tracker ("Application") values your privacy. This document explains how your data is handled when you use the app.

1. Data Storage (Local Storage)

This application is built with a "Privacy-First" approach. Any expenses, subscription amounts, or categories you enter are NOT transmitted to any remote servers. All your data is stored locally in your browser's LocalStorage, strictly on your own device.

2. Personal Data

The Application does not require registration. It does not collect, process, or share personally identifiable information such as names, emails, phone numbers, or banking details.

3. Third-Party Services

This app is hosted via GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect standard access logs (IP address, browser type, etc.) for security and operational purposes. Please refer to the GitHub Privacy Statement for more details.

4. Data Control

You have full control over your data. Clearing your browser cache or using the "Reset Data" option within the app will permanently delete all your entries.
